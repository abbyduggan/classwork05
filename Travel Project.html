Marieta's Beautiful Hidden Beach

Why not take a trip down to Mexico and visit the gorgeous hidden beach of Marieta? This vacation spot is covered by a sandy cavern with a large hole facing the sky, but look out! You might miss it! It is completely invisible from the outside; title="A view of the beach." /> it can only be accessed through a water tunnel that links the beach to the Pacific Ocean. Its strange shape can be linked to bomb testings in the early 1900s, thought to be an ideal spot uninhabited by the Mexican people. Ideal for canoeing and kayaking, this hidden beach is one thing you don't want to miss. title="A woman sitting on the beach." /> Go to this link for more information: Atlas Obscura title="A beautiful image of the sandy cavern of the hidden beach." />
